ARK – Allied Relic Keeper

Your all-in-one device for monitoring environmental conditions, motion, and location of artifacts. This guide will help you get started and make the most of ARK.

Getting Started

  • Power on the ARK device using the main switch.
  • Connect it to Wi-Fi or mobile network to start transmitting data.
  • Install the ARK dashboard app on your computer or mobile device.
  • Login using your assigned credentials.

Sensors

ARK includes temperature, humidity, and air quality sensors. Here's how to read the data:

  • Temperature: Read in °C, updated every 2 seconds.
  • Humidity: Percent humidity of surrounding environment.
  • Air Quality: AQI index showing particulate matter levels.

Motion & Alerts

Motion sensors detect artifact movement or container opening. Alerts notify you in real-time.

  • Motion detected → instant alert on dashboard.
  • Box opened → log timestamp automatically.
  • Artifact removed or returned → updated in motion logs.

Map & Location

View the ARK device’s GPS location on a map. You can track its current position and historical movements.

FAQ & Tips

  • Q: Device not sending data?
    A: Ensure Wi-Fi or network is active.
  • Q: Motion alerts not received?
    A: Check your notification settings.
  • Q: How to reset device?
    A: Hold the power button for 10 seconds.
