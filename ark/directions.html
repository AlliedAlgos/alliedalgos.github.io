DIY Artifact Brick Instructions

Step 1: Print the Box

3D print this box according to your needed size and dimensions.

Step 2: Buy Components

Purchase the Arduino and sensors needed — total around $40.

Step 3: Assemble It

Place the electronics inside the 3D printed box exactly as shown.

Step 4: Seal Into Brick

Place your artifact inside, then fill with spray foam to harden into a brick.
